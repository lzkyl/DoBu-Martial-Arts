Program Overview

We offer a variety of martial arts and fitness programs for all levels and age groups.

Fitness Program

Our fitness programs cater to individuals of all levels. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned athlete, we focus on building strength, cardiovascular health, flexibility, and overall wellness.

  • Strength Training: Build muscle and endurance with free weights, bodyweight exercises, and resistance training.
  • Cardio Workouts: Boost your heart health and burn calories with HIIT, circuit training, and more.
  • Flexibility & Mobility: Improve your range of motion and prevent injury with stretching and mobility drills.
  • Group Fitness Classes: Join a supportive community to stay motivated.
Martial Arts

Our martial arts programs offer both physical and mental growth. Whether you're looking for a traditional martial arts experience or modern combat sports, we have something for everyone.

  • Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ): Learn grappling skills, submission techniques, and ground control.
  • Muay Thai: Improve striking, conditioning, and self-defense techniques with this art of eight limbs.
  • Boxing: Master footwork, defense, and punching techniques.
  • Kickboxing: Boost your cardio and strength with high-energy training.
Self-Defense

Our self-defense program teaches practical, real-world techniques for staying safe. Build your confidence, learn how to stay calm under pressure, and become aware of potential threats.

  • Street-Smart Defense: Learn basic self-defense skills for everyday situations.
  • Situational Awareness: Recognize and avoid potential dangers.
  • Escape Techniques: Break free from holds or grabs in real-life scenarios.
  • Confidence Building: Develop mental toughness to handle emergencies.
Adult Programs

Our adult programs are designed to help you achieve your fitness and self-defense goals. Whether you're looking to learn new skills or stay active, we have flexible classes to fit your needs.

  • Mixed Fitness & Martial Arts: Strength training, cardio, and martial arts combined for maximum results.
  • Self-Defense for Adults: Practical self-defense techniques for personal safety.
  • Stress Relief & Mental Focus: Engage in activities that improve mental clarity and reduce stress.

Junior Programs

Our junior programs introduce children to martial arts and fitness in a safe and supportive environment. These classes promote discipline, respect, and confidence.

  • Kids Martial Arts: Basic martial arts techniques in a fun and energetic setting.
  • Youth Self-Defense: Empower children with skills to protect themselves and resolve conflicts.
  • Fitness for Kids: Encourage healthy habits through physical activity and fun fitness challenges.
  • After-School Programs: Structured programs that promote growth and fitness outside school hours.

Program Overview

Jiu-jitsu

Description: Brazilian Jiu-jitsu (BJJ) focuses on ground fighting, submissions, and grappling. Techniques include joint locks, chokeholds, and positional control. Classes are available for all levels, from beginner to advanced.

Duration: 1.5 to 2 hours.

Booking: Available for members at all levels (Basic, Intermediate, Advanced, Elite).

Karate

Description: Traditional Karate emphasizes striking techniques and self-defense. Classes include punches, kicks, and forms (Kata). Available for all levels, including children.

Duration: 1.5 hours.

Booking: Open to all membership levels.

Muay Thai

Description: Muay Thai, known as the "Art of Eight Limbs," focuses on striking using fists, elbows, knees, and shins. The program includes conditioning, pad work, and sparring.

Duration: 1.5 hours.

Booking: Available for Intermediate to Elite members.

Judo

Description: Judo is a grappling-based martial art that emphasizes throws, joint locks, and pins. It is ideal for both self-defense and sport competition.

Duration: 1.5 hours.
